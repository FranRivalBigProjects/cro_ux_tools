❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is conversion rate optimization (CRO)?

CRO is the process of improving your website to increase the percentage of visitors who take a desired action.

Why do users abandon carts?

Common reasons include high costs, complicated checkout, lack of trust, or poor UX.

How can I improve my landing page?

Focus on clear messaging, strong CTAs, fast loading speed, and trust signals.

Are these tools useful for SaaS?

Yes. CRO tools are critical for SaaS pricing pages, onboarding flows, and conversions.
